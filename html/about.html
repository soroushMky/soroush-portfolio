About Me

Design meets Data.

I didn't start out doing one thing — I started doing everything. Designing logos. Building websites. Running campaigns. Crunching data. Somewhere in that chaos I found my edge: I speak the language of both design and data, and I use both to build products that actually work.

Over 6+ years I've worked across UI/UX, product design, graphic art, and data analytics — contributing to brands like RUDSAK, Boutique TAG, Reno Viva, and Parto Ads. I've redesigned e‑commerce interfaces that lifted mobile conversion by 12%, built accessible healthcare portals that reduced support inquiries by 20%, and delivered data dashboards that turned raw numbers into real decisions.

My background in Electrical & Computer Engineering (MEng, Concordia) means I don't just design how things look — I think about how they work, scale, and perform. Whether you're a startup needing a designer who can also read your data, or a team looking for someone who bridges creativity and logic — I'm ready.
